Suggested travel to Linköping

•     Your Location è Amsterdam è Linköping City Airport

Flights to Linköping City Airport only depart from Amsterdam. Tickets are available through KLM.

•     Stockholm Arlanda Airport

Stockholm Arlanda Airport has direct flights from most international airports in Europe. From there, Linköping is reachable by bus or by train (e.g.

https://www.omio.com/trains/stockholm-arlanda/linkoeping or https://www.sj.se/en).

•     Stockholm Skavsta Airport

Stockholm Skavsta Airport is located 100 km north of Linköping, and is only 90 minutes away by buses booked through e.g. Flygbussarna

(https://www.flygbussarna.se/en/) and FlixBus (https://www.flixbus.ie/).

 

 

Suggested travel within Linköping

•     Östgötatrafiken

Tickets for Linköping’s public transport Östgötatrafiken can be purchased through their app (available in App Store/Google Play by the same name), or by paying with your debit/credit card (Visa/MasterCard) onboard.

 

Linköping University Campus Valla is close to bus stops: Universitetet, Mäster Mattias väg, Solhaga, and Nobeltorget. You can use Google Maps for the best options.

The venue will be in the B-huset of the Campus Valla, more information will be provided later.

•     Other means of transportation

There are several options, e.g., taxis (expensive), Uber, and renting electric scooters (cheap).
